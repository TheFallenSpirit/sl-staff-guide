## Admin Duties
The duties of our admins in Starlight.

### Helper & Moderator Intervention
It is our admins' job to periodically monitor our helpers and moderators to make sure they are properly staffing.
If a lower staff member isn't staffing properly, or is causing issues, you can jail them and talk to them about their behaviour.

If you believe a staff member needs demotion or removal, notify a member of the Eternal Overlords.
Admins aren't allowed to remove or demote staff members, only jail them, and moderate them like you would a normal member.

### Event Management
If events are planned, it is our admins' job to coordinate and schedule these events.
Public events must have a native Discord event created for them,
and that event must be started by an admin or event manager when it's time.

### Staff Team Management
It's our admins' job to manage lower staff members.
If you believe a member needs to be promoted or demoted, consult with your fellow admins and reach a consensus.

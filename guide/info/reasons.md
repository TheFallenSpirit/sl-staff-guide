# Predefined Reasons
Here is a list of predefined reasons that should be used when moderating users.

- `minor` - Suspected of being a minor or is a confirmed minor.
- `frq`, `friend` - Sent a friend request to a member without asking while they had the "DMs Closed" or "Ask to DM" role.
- `dm`, `message` - Sent a direct message to a member without asking while they had the "DMs Closed" or "Ask to DM" role.
- `unv`, `unverified` - Sent another member a DM or friend request while unverified.
- `play` - Disrespecting another member's play roles.
- `touch` - Disrespecting another member's touch roles.
- `nsfw` - Posting NSFW content in an non age-restricted channel.
- `adv`, `advertisement` - Self advertising a Discord server or paid service in your bio or in a message.
- `slur` - Using a slur in a chat or voice chat. Slurs are strictly prohibited in our server.
- `jail`, `ticket` - Failing to respond to a jail or ticket within 24 hours.
- `warns`, `threshold` - You have reached 3 or more recent warns and have been automatically banned.
- `bot` - Your account is suspected of being a bot/non-genuine account.
- `spam`, `flood` - Sending excessive messages, large walls of text, or frequently using bot commands outside of bot channels.

# Staff Policies
A list of our current staff policies, and what they're for.

### Staff Feedback
Staff members are encouraged to provide constructive criticism of actions taken by higher up staff.
If you think something could have been done better, respectfully say something.
No one is perfect, and there's always ways to improve on our handling of issues and the server.

### Unannounced Leave Policy
If you leave the server without notice or warning, you will not get your staff roles back on join. You will have to re-apply for staff.

### Staff Removal Timeout
If you leave the staff team, either via demotion or voluntarily, you must wait 3 months before you re-apply for staff.
